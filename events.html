Technology & Engineering Design
Boomilever
📅 Apr 5, 2025 🏗️ Build Event 👥 Team of 2

A classic engineering challenge, Boomilever requires teams to design and construct a lightweight structure, called a boomilever, capable of supporting a significant load. The boomilever is typically made of wood and must adhere to specific size and weight constraints. Competitors are scored based on the structure's efficiency, which is the ratio of the mass it can hold to the mass of the boomilever itself. This event emphasizes structural design principles, engineering optimization, and precision in construction.

Ping Pong Parachute
📅 Apr 26, 2025 ⚙️ Build Event 👥 Team of 2

Ping Pong Parachute is a high-flying build event where teams design and construct bottle rockets that launch a ping pong ball attached to a parachute. The goal? Keep the ball suspended in the air as long as possible. Competitors must optimize aerodynamics, parachute deployment, and overall build performance under strict constraints. It’s all about physics, timing, and that perfect slow descent—think NASA meets Dollar Tree engineering.
